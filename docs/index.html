The non-profit DrupalNYC Incorporated and the DrupalNYC community proudly present DrupalCampNYC 2021!

Connect with hundreds of members of the Drupal community from the tri-state area and beyond to collaborate, share knowledge, see old friends, make new friends, find new business opportunities, and help grow the Drupal project.

Who should attend?

  • People new to Drupal
  • Web developers looking to up their game
  • Graphic artists and designers
  • Project managers
  • Site owners
  • Content managers
  • Marketing professionals
  • Those seeking a powerful open-source CMS

Dates and format

October 28-30, online plus an in-person social event in NYC.

We had hoped to offer a truly hybrid conference with most programming available both in-person and online. However, due to the Delta variant's recent rise and related uncertainties about our venue's availability, we made the tough decision to pivot to a primarily online conference to allow the organizers, speakers, and sponsors to plan confidently and everyone to attend safely.

What to expect

  • New York City flavor and attitude! How you doin’?!?
  • Great community
  • Hands-on trainings
  • Engaging sessions
  • An enlightening DevOps Summit
  • An in-person social hour
  • A virtual social hour
  • Lots of Drupal fun!

Tickets and cost

FREE*

*Thanks to the generosity of our sponsors, we are thrilled to be able to offer all attendees free general admission tickets to DrupalCampNYC 2021! We encourage attendees who are able to make a donation to our non-profit, DrupalNYC Incorporated, when reserving tickets.

To reduce no-shows for our trainings, which have limited capacities, we will charge attendees a deposit for each training ($25 for half day trainings and $50 for full-day trainings), which will be fully refundable once they participate in the training, or which can be donated to our non-profit to support our educational and scientific mission.

We will waive the refundable deposit requirement for anyone for whom it would create hardship (please email camp@drupalnyc.org).
